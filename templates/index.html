📚 What do these fields mean?

Symboling — insurance risk rating assigned by actuaries. Higher = riskier.
Aspiration — "std" means naturally aspirated; "turbo" means turbocharged engine.
Wheelbase — distance between front and rear axles. Longer = more stable.
Curb Weight — weight of the car without passengers or cargo.
Bore Ratio — diameter of each cylinder; affects engine performance.
MPFI / 2bbl — fuel injection types. MPFI is modern multi-point injection.
